Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

Our guiding principle: We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

AIT Inc Ltd (“we,” “our,” or “us”) develops and publishes apps, games, and software under the domain aitinc.site. Protecting your privacy is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and what information we do and do not collect.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our software and website are intentionally designed so that we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). This means we do not request, store, or process data such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location or GPS data
  • IP addresses linked to identity
  • Any other data that could be used to identify you personally

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We do not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data collection tools. Our software operates independently and does not transmit your data to external servers or services.

4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Some of our apps and games may store data locally on your device such as preferences, settings, or save files to improve your experience. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling it.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our software is suitable for general audiences and does not target children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data, no child specific information is gathered, stored, or shared.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. Any significant changes will be posted here with a new “Effective Date.” If future updates involve data collection, we will clearly describe what data is collected, why it’s collected, and how it’s protected before such changes take effect.
